Luna pizza

Description

This pizza recipe we will be making is a supreme pizza meaning it will have various vegetable ingredients like mushrooms and bell peppers

it also includes different type of meats like Italian ground beef and sausage.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Cornmeal
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Green bell pepper
  • Yellow bell pepper
  • Purple onion
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Italian ground beef
  • Sausage

Steps

  1. Add 2 Cups of flour in a bowl
  2. Add 1 Tbsp of activated yeast to the bowl
  3. Add 1 Tbsp of sugar to the bowl
  4. Add 1 Tbsp of salt to the bowl
  5. Add 2 Tbsp of olive oil to the bowl
  6. Add 3/4 Cup of water to the bowl
  7. Mix the ingredients until the it becomes a soft dough
  8. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to a 12 inch diameter saucer
  9. Flip the dough to its underside and spread a light layer of oil followed by cornmeal to the pizza's underside
  10. Turn on the oven and set to 435 degrees
  11. Cut the vegetables and shred the cheeses and prepare the meats to be added
  12. Put the pizza in the oven for 5 minutes
  13. Take the pizza out and add 1/2 a cup of pizza sauce on its surface
  14. Add 8 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese to the pizza
  15. Add pepperoni slices to the pizza
  16. Add all the vegetables on the pizza
  17. Add all the meats to the pizza
  18. Place the pizza in the top rack and take it out after 20 minutes

Enjoy!
