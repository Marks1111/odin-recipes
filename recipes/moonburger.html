Moon burger

Description

This burger will have a quarter pound of ground beef with various toppings and mozzarella cheese

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Purple Onion
  • Dill pickles
  • Burger buns
  • Tomato slices
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Shape a quarter pound worth of ground beef and shape it into a patty
  2. Slice off a piece of mozzarella
  3. Cut all the vegetables and prepare the lettuce and pickles
  4. Fry the burger patty for 3 minutes on medium each side
  5. Heat the buns in a grill pan until light brown
  6. Stack the burger and apply your favorite condiments

Enjoy!
