Lunar Taco

Description

These tacos will be made out of ground beef mixed with cut green bell peppers and a four mexican white cheese blend along with various toppings

Ingredients

  • Hard shell taco *Pre made*
  • Ground beef
  • Four cheese white mexican blend
  • Tomatos
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Green bell peppers

Steps

  1. Cut up the green peppers and onion into squares and cook in a pan on low heat with a bit of oil for 2 minutes
  2. Place the ground beef in the same pan on medium heat for 7 minutes keep stirring
  3. Slice the lettuce and tomatos
  4. Prepare the hard shell tacos and place some of the shredded cheese blend on the bottom
  5. Add all the ground beef to the hard shelled taco and then all the toppings
  6. Add favorite condiments

Enjoy!
